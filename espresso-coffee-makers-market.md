<p><p>Espresso coffee makers are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require users to control every aspect of the brewing process, offering complete customization and skill development. Semi-automatic models provide a balance, allowing users to manage the grind and tamping while automating water flow. Fully automatic machines simplify the process further by automating both the brewing and grinding, catering to those seeking convenience without sacrificing quality. Each type appeals to different preferences and skill levels in coffee preparation.</p></p>
