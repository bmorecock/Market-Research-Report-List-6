<p><p>Berthing Aid Systems are essential for assisting vessels during docking and undocking procedures, enhancing safety and efficiency. Laser-based systems utilize precise laser beams to measure distances, providing accurate positional feedback to ship operators, ideal for tight marinas. Both technologies improve navigation and reduce collision risks.</p></p>
