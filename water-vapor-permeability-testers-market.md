<p><strong>The Water Vapor Permeability Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravimetric Method(Cup Method)</li><li>Electrolytic Detection Sensor Method</li><li>Infrared Detection Sensor Method</li><li>Humidity Detection Sensor Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water vapor permeability testers are categorized based on their measurement techniques. The gravimetric method, also known as the cup method, quantifies the weight change of a material as water vapor passes through it. The electrolytic detection sensor method utilizes electrochemical reactions to detect moisture levels. The infrared detection sensor method measures the absorption of infrared light by water vapor, while the humidity detection sensor method assesses the relative humidity changes in the surrounding environment, ensuring diverse applications in material testing.</p></p>
