PWRs use pressurized water as both coolant and moderator, ensuring efficient heat transfer. WWERs, primarily developed in Russia, utilize water for both cooling and neutron moderation, offering robust safety features. PHWRs employ heavy water, allowing for fuel flexibility and reduced enrichment costs. Each type plays a critical role in meeting global energy demands while emphasizing safety and efficiency.</p></p>
