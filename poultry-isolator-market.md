<p><strong>The Poultry Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure Isolator</li><li>Negative Pressure Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The poultry isolator market is divided into two main types: positive pressure isolators and negative pressure isolators. Positive pressure isolators create a barrier by maintaining higher internal pressure, preventing contaminants from entering, making them ideal for hatching and breeding operations. Conversely, negative pressure isolators maintain a lower internal pressure, effectively containing potential pathogens, making them suitable for biosecurity applications where strict control over disease spread is crucial. Both types enhance poultry health and productivity through controlled environments.</p></p>
