<p><strong>The Foldable Phones Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-folding Type</li><li>Out-folding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foldable phones market comprises mainly two types: in-folding and out-folding. In-folding phones feature a screen that folds inward, providing protection to the display and a compact design. This type often allows for a more seamless user experience when unfolded. In contrast, out-folding phones have screens that fold outward, exposing the display when closed. This design can lead to a larger screen area but may increase the risk of damage. Both types aim to enhance portability while offering innovative features.</p></p>
