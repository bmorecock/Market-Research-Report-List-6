<p><ul><li>Standard Tablet Coating Pan</li><li>Perforated Tablet Coating Pan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coating pans market is primarily divided into two types: standard tablet coating pans and perforated tablet coating pans. Standard tablet coating pans are designed for uniform coating of tablets using a rotating drum mechanism, ensuring even distribution of coating materials. In contrast, perforated tablet coating pans feature holes that enhance airflow and allow moisture escape, improving drying efficiency. Both types are essential in the pharmaceutical industry for producing coated tablets with desired aesthetics, stability, and controlled release properties.</p></p>
