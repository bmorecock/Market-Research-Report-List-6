<p><p>The automotive washer pumps market is primarily categorized into mono and dual pump types. Mono pumps consist of a single motor-driven unit, designed for simplicity and cost-effectiveness, commonly used for basic windshield washer systems. In contrast, dual pumps feature two separate units, allowing for enhanced functionality, such as simultaneous operation for front and rear washers or different fluid types. The choice between mono and dual pumps often depends on vehicle specifications, desired performance, and consumer preferences for convenience and efficiency.</p></p>
