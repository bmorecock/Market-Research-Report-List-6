<p><p>The residential air cooler market comprises two primary types: portable air coolers and fixed air coolers. Portable air coolers are versatile and can be easily moved from one room to another, making them ideal for renters or those needing temporary cooling solutions. In contrast, fixed air coolers are installed in a specific location, providing consistent cooling for larger areas over a longer period. Both types use evaporative cooling technology but cater to different consumer needs based on mobility and installation preferences.</p></p>
