<p><strong>The Borehole Enlargement System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-diameter Reamers</li><li>Hydraulically Expandable Reamers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Borehole Enlargement System market comprises two primary types: fixed-diameter reamers and hydraulically expandable reamers. Fixed-diameter reamers are designed to create a consistent borehole diameter, ensuring precise drilling for various applications. In contrast, hydraulically expandable reamers offer flexibility, allowing the borehole diameter to be adjusted during drilling operations. This adaptability makes them suitable for a wider range of geological conditions and project requirements, enhancing efficiency and reducing operational costs in drilling projects.</p></p>
