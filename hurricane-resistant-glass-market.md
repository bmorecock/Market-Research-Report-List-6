<p><p>The hurricane-resistant glass market primarily features two types: laminated glass and insulated laminated glass. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers of glass bonded with a interlayer, providing enhanced strength and shatter resistance. Insulated laminated glass combines laminated glass with a thermal insulating layer, improving energy efficiency and noise reduction while retaining structural integrity during severe weather events. Both types offer vital protection against high winds and debris, making them essential for coastal and hurricane-prone regions.</p></p>
