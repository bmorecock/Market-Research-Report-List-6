<p><strong>The NFC Product Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NFC Controller</li><li>NFC Tag</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NFC product market primarily consists of two segments: NFC controllers and NFC tags. NFC controllers are integrated circuits that manage communication between devices, enabling secure transactions and data exchanges, commonly found in smartphones and wearables. In contrast, NFC tags are passive devices that store information and respond when in proximity to an NFC reader. They are widely used in applications like access control, ticketing, and marketing, enhancing user interaction through seamless connectivity and data sharing in various environments.</p></p>
