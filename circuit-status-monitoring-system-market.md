<p><p>Circuit Status Monitoring Systems are categorized into two primary market types: Independent Monitoring and Centralized Monitoring. Independent Monitoring systems operate autonomously, allowing for localized tracking of circuit conditions without reliance on central control. These systems are typically deployed in remote or critical areas. In contrast, Centralized Monitoring systems aggregate data from multiple circuits at a central point, enabling comprehensive oversight and management. This approach facilitates real-time analysis and quick decision-making, improving overall reliability and operational efficiency.</p></p>
