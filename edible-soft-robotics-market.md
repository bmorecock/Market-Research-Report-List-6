<p><p>The Edible Soft Robotics market is primarily divided into two types: Solid Structure Robots and Fluid Structure Robots. Solid Structure Robots are designed with rigid components, often utilizing edible materials to create stable forms that can perform specific tasks. In contrast, Fluid Structure Robots rely on flexible, fluid-filled designs that mimic natural movements, allowing for adaptability and versatility. Both types leverage innovative materials to create functional, safe, and eco-friendly solutions in applications such as food handling, packaging, and interactive dining experiences.</p></p>
