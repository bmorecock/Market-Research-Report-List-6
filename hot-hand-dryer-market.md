<p><strong>The Hot Hand Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hand-in</li><li>Hand-under</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot hand dryer market is primarily divided into two types: hand-in and hand-under dryers. Hand-in dryers require users to insert their hands into a protective enclosure for drying, offering focused airflow and often enhanced hygiene. In contrast, hand-under dryers allow users to place their hands beneath a nozzle, where powerful jets of air dry them from above. Both types cater to different user preferences, with hand-in models promoting hygiene and hand-under models emphasizing convenience and speed.</p></p>
