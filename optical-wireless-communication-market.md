<p><p>The optical wireless communication market encompasses three primary types: visible light communication (VLC), infrared communication (IRC), and ultraviolet communication (UVC). VLC utilizes visible light for data transmission, offering high bandwidth and security in environments like offices and hospitals. Infrared communication employs undetectable infrared signals, ideal for short-range applications like remote controls and wireless data transfer in personal devices. Ultraviolet communication, although less common, leverages UV light for specialized applications such as secure communications and environmental monitoring, providing unique advantages in specific scenarios.</p></p>
