<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membranes</li><li>High Flux Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic hollow-fiber dialyzer market is divided into two main types: low flux membranes and high flux membranes. Low flux membranes allow the passage of small molecules and are typically used for patients with stable renal function, providing effective waste removal. In contrast, high flux membranes facilitate the clearance of larger molecules, making them suitable for patients with more complex needs or those undergoing intensive dialysis. Each type serves distinct patient requirements, influencing adoption rates and market growth.</p></p>
