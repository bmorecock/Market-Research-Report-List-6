<p><ul><li>Active Monitor Speaker</li><li>Passive Monitor Speaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stage monitor speaker market is divided into two main types: active and passive monitor speakers. Active monitor speakers contain built-in amplifiers, offering a simplified setup and the convenience of easy volume control. They deliver consistent performance, often featuring integrated digital signal processing for enhanced sound quality. Conversely, passive monitor speakers require external amplifiers, allowing for greater customization of sound systems. They offer flexibility in configuration but involve more complexity in setup, appealing to users who prefer tailored audio experiences.</p></p>
