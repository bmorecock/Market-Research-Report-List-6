<p><p>The belt scales market comprises three primary types: single-idler, two-idler, and multi-idler scales. Single-idler scales feature one sensor for measuring weight, best suited for simpler applications. Two-idler scales use two sensors for improved accuracy and are ideal for medium-duty tasks. Multi-idler scales incorporate multiple sensors to provide precise weight measurements across a wide range of materials, making them suitable for high-capacity or complex applications. Each type caters to different accuracy, capacity, and operational requirements.</p></p>
