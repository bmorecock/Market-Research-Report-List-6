<p><ul><li>Ablative Laser</li><li>Nonablative Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical laser resurfacing treatment devices are categorized into two primary types: ablative and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove the outer layers of skin, promoting significant skin tightening and rejuvenation, making them effective for deep wrinkles and scars. In contrast, non-ablative lasers work beneath the skin’s surface, stimulating collagen production with minimal disruption to the skin’s outer layer, which results in less downtime and milder effects, suitable for treating superficial skin issues. Both types cater to diverse patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
