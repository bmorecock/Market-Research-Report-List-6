<p><p>The Neodymium Iron Boron (NdFeB) magnet market is primarily divided into two types: bonded NdFeB and sintered NdFeB. Bonded NdFeB magnets are made by combining powdered NdFeB with a polymer matrix, allowing for complex shapes and higher magnetic performance in applications like electronics and automotive components. Sintered NdFeB magnets, produced through a high-temperature sintering process, offer superior magnetic properties and are commonly used in industrial applications, such as motors and generators, due to their strength and durability.</p></p>
