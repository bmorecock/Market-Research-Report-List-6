<p><p>The gas leak detector market includes various types, each with distinct sensing technologies. Electrochemical detectors utilize chemical reactions to identify gas presence, offering high sensitivity for specific gases. Semiconductor detectors rely on changes in electrical resistance to detect leaks and are cost-effective. Ultrasonic detectors sense the sound of gas leaks, making them suitable for noisy environments. Infrared detectors measure gas absorption of infrared light, providing accurate detection for various gases. Other technologies may include thermal conductivity and photoionization methods, expanding application versatility.</p></p>
