Blade type sensors utilize rotating blades to measure airflow, providing precise readings and responsive performance. Damper type sensors control airflow using adjustable dampers, enabling efficient air management in HVAC systems. Other types may include various configurations and technologies such as thermal or ultrasonic sensors. Each type serves specific applications, offering varying levels of accuracy, response times, and installation requirements suited to different environments.</p></p>
