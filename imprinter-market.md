<p><p>The Imprinter market encompasses various printing technologies. Relief printing presses use raised surfaces to transfer ink, ideal for high-volume jobs. Planographic presses operate on a flat surface principle, notably lithography, suited for detailed images. Intaglio printing presses engrave designs into a substrate, delivering high-quality prints, often used in currency and fine art. Screen-process printing presses use stencils and a mesh to transfer ink, highly effective for textiles and graphics, allowing vibrant colors and intricate designs. Each type serves distinct applications and markets.</p></p>
