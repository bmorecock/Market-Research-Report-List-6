<p><p>The Single Zone Variable Air Volume (VAV) system market includes two main types: Single-Duct and Dual-Duct systems. Single-Duct VAV systems utilize one duct to deliver conditioned air to a space, adjusting air volume to meet varying thermal loads efficiently. In contrast, Dual-Duct systems feature two separate ducts for hot and cold air, allowing for more precise temperature control by mixing the two streams as needed. Both systems offer energy efficiency, but the choice depends on specific building requirements and climate conditions.</p></p>
