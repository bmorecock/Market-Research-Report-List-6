<p><strong>The PC and PMMA Composite Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-layer</li><li>Three-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PC and PMMA composite sheet market is categorized into two main types: two-layer and three-layer sheets. Two-layer composites consist of a polycarbonate (PC) layer sandwiched between two PMMA layers, offering a balance of strength and clarity. In contrast, three-layer sheets add an additional PC or PMMA layer for improved impact resistance and thermal insulation. These configurations cater to diverse applications, including signage, glazing, and automotive components, leveraging their unique properties for enhanced performance and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
