<p><strong>The Marine Reinsurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proportional Reinsurance</li><li>Non-proportional Reinsurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine reinsurance market consists of two primary types: proportional and non-proportional reinsurance. Proportional reinsurance involves sharing a percentage of premiums and losses between the insurer and reinsurer, allowing for risk distribution and stability. Conversely, non-proportional reinsurance triggers coverage only when losses exceed a specified threshold, offering protection against significant, unexpected losses. Together, these types help insurers manage risks associated with maritime operations while enhancing their capacity to underwrite high-value policies in a volatile environment.</p></p>
