<p><p>The fiberglass insulation market comprises three main types: continuous fiber, fixed-length fiber, and glass wool. Continuous fiber insulation is made from long strands of glass fibers, offering high thermal resistance and flexibility. Fixed-length fiber insulation consists of shorter strands, providing effective thermal control while being easier to handle. Glass wool, made from fine glass fibers, is lightweight and highly effective for thermal and acoustic insulation. Each type caters to specific application needs and preferences in construction and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
