<p><p>The immunochemistry analyzer market is diverse, featuring various types of analyzers. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) analyzers utilize light emissions for detection, while Immunofluorescence (IFA) analyzers employ fluorescent dyes. Enzyme Immunoassay (EIA) analyzers use enzyme-substrate reactions, and Radioimmunoassay (RIA) analyzers depend on radioactive isotopes. Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Assay (ELFA) systems combine enzyme and fluorescent detection methods, and Multiplexed Assay Systems enable simultaneous testing for multiple targets, enhancing diagnostic efficiency and accuracy across clinical applications.</p></p>
