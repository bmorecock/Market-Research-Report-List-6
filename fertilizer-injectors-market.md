<p><p>Fertilizer injectors in the market can be categorized into two main types: constant pressure and non-constant pressure injectors. Constant pressure injectors maintain a uniform pressure for consistent nutrient delivery, ensuring reliable performance in various applications. In contrast, non-constant pressure injectors adjust their flow based on varying system pressures, allowing for flexibility but potentially leading to inconsistent nutrient application. Each type serves distinct needs in agricultural practices, influencing efficiency, precision, and overall crop yield outcomes.</p></p>
