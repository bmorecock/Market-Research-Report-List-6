<p><p>The sulfur recovery technology market primarily includes two key types: the Claus process and tail gas treatment. The Claus process efficiently converts hydrogen sulfide from sour gas into elemental sulfur, typically achieving high recovery rates. Following this, tail gas treatment further processes remaining gases to ensure comprehensive sulfur recovery and minimize emissions. Together, these technologies play a critical role in refining operations, environmental compliance, and sustainable resource management, supporting industries in meeting stringent pollution control regulations.</p></p>
