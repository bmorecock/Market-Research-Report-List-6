<p><ul><li>Axial Flow</li><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine ventilator market consists of several types, including axial flow, centrifugal, and other specialized designs. Axial flow ventilators are efficient for high-volume air movement in confined spaces, using blades to push air parallel to the axis. Centrifugal ventilators, on the other hand, utilize a rotating impeller to draw air and force it radially, providing increased pressure for ventilating larger areas or overcoming system resistance. Other market types may include hybrid designs and custom solutions tailored for specific marine applications, enhancing overall ventilation efficiency.</p></p>
