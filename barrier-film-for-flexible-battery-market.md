<p><ul><li>Multilayer Barrier (MLB) Film Technology</li><li>Thin Film Encapsulation (TFE) Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Barrier Film for Flexible Battery market primarily encompasses two technologies: Multilayer Barrier (MLB) Film Technology and Thin Film Encapsulation (TFE) Technology. MLB films consist of multiple layers that enhance moisture and gas resistance, prolonging battery life and performance. TFE technology utilizes ultra-thin films to encapsulate battery components, providing effective protection against environmental factors while maintaining flexibility. Both technologies are essential for improving the durability and reliability of flexible batteries used in various applications such as wearables and portable electronics.</p></p>
