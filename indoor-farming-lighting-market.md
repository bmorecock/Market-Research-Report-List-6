<p><p>The indoor farming lighting market is primarily divided into two types: halogen and LED. Halogen lights, known for their high-intensity output and warm color temperature, provide effective illumination but consume more energy and generate significant heat. In contrast, LED lights have gained popularity due to their energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and the ability to emit specific light spectra beneficial for plant growth. As sustainability becomes a priority, LED technology is increasingly favored for its lower operational costs and minimal heat production.</p></p>
