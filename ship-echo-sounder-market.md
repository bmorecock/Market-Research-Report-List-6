<p><ul><li>Dual Frequency Echo Sounder</li><li>Single Frequency Echo Sounder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ship Echo Sounder market is primarily divided into two types: Dual Frequency Echo Sounders and Single Frequency Echo Sounders. Dual Frequency Echo Sounders utilize two different frequencies to provide detailed depth profiling and distinguish between various underwater features, making them ideal for complex marine environments. In contrast, Single Frequency Echo Sounders operate at one frequency, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution for straightforward depth measurements and basic navigational needs. Both types are essential for accurate marine surveying and navigation.</p></p>
