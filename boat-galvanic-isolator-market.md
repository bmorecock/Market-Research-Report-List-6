<p><p>The Boat Galvanic Isolator market can be categorized into two main types: Signal Isolation and Power Isolation. Signal Isolation prevents harmful galvanic currents from affecting communication signals, ensuring reliable operation of onboard electronic systems. Power Isolation, on the other hand, protects electrical power systems from galvanic corrosion by isolating the shore power supply from the boat's electrical circuits. Both types are critical for enhancing safety and longevity of marine vessels by reducing the risk of corrosion and electrical faults.</p></p>
