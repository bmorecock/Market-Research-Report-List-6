<p><p>The Automatic Vehicle Fire Suppression System market primarily consists of two types: wet and dry systems. Wet systems utilize water or a water-based extinguishing agent to suppress fires through spray nozzles, providing rapid cooling and extinguishment. In contrast, dry systems employ dry chemical agents or foam to smother flames, which are effective in high-temperature environments. Both systems are designed to enhance vehicle safety by minimizing fire risks, with applications in various vehicles, including buses, trucks, and emergency response vehicles.</p></p>
