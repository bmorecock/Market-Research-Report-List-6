<p><p>The biomass transformer oil market primarily consists of two types: natural transformer fluid and synthetic transformer oil. Natural transformer fluid is derived from renewable biomass sources, offering environmentally friendly characteristics and lower ecological impact. In contrast, synthetic transformer oil is artificially produced, often featuring enhanced thermal stability and electrical properties. Both types serve as insulating fluids in transformers, ensuring efficient energy transmission while aligning with sustainability goals. Their applications are critical in modern power systems, focusing on reducing carbon footprints.</p></p>
