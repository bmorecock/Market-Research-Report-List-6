<p><p>The Negative Feedback Circuit Linear Regulator IC market is primarily divided into two types: Series Type and Shunt Type. </p><p>Series Type regulators control the output voltage by varying the resistance in series with the load, providing high efficiency and better performance under varying loads. In contrast, Shunt Type regulators maintain a constant voltage by diverting excess current away from the load, often featuring simpler designs and lower cost. Each type serves specific applications based on efficiency requirements, load conditions, and design complexity.</p></p>
