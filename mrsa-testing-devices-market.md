<p><p>The MRSA testing devices market encompasses three key types: immunodiagnostics, molecular diagnostics, and point-of-care (POC) testing. Immunodiagnostics use antibody-based methods to detect MRSA, offering quick results suitable for laboratory settings. Molecular diagnostics utilize techniques like PCR to identify MRSA’s genetic material, providing higher sensitivity and specificity. POC testing allows for rapid MRSA detection at the bedside or in outpatient settings, enhancing immediate clinical decision-making. Together, these methods address the urgent need for efficient MRSA screening and infection control.</p></p>
