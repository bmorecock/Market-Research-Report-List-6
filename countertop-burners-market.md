<p><p>Countertop burners are available in three primary types: gas, electric, and induction. Gas burners provide immediate heat and precise temperature control, making them popular for traditional cooking. Electric burners, often coil or smooth-top, offer easy cleaning and consistent heat distribution but may take longer to heat up. Induction burners use magnetic fields to heat cookware directly, ensuring fast cooking and energy efficiency while remaining cooler to the touch. Each type caters to different cooking preferences and needs.</p></p>
