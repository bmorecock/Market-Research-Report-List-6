<p><strong>The Power Line Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Mode Interference Current</li><li>Common Mode Interference Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Line Filters market is divided into two main types based on interference characteristics: Differential Mode Interference Current and Common Mode Interference Current. Differential Mode Filters primarily target noise that occurs between the live and neutral lines, often resulting from switching power supplies and electronic devices. In contrast, Common Mode Filters address noise that flows in the same direction on both live and neutral lines, typically caused by external electromagnetic interference. Each type serves distinct applications, enhancing power quality and device performance.</p></p>
