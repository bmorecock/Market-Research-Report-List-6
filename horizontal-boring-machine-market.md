<p><p>Horizontal boring machines (HBMs) are categorized into three main market types: table, planer, and floor. </p><p>Table boring machines are compact and designed for smaller workpieces, often used in workshops for light to moderate tasks. Planer HBMs feature a larger table to accommodate more extensive components, suitable for machining operations requiring precision. Floor boring machines are robust and designed for heavy-duty applications, allowing large workpieces to be manipulated easily. Each type serves distinct applications, depending on the size and complexity of the tasks involved.</p></p>
