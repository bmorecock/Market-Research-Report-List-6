<p><ul><li>Steam Thermal Weeder</li><li>Flame Thermal Weeder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal weeder market primarily comprises two types: steam thermal weeders and flame thermal weeders. Steam thermal weeders utilize high-pressure steam to kill unwanted plants by disrupting their cellular structure, making them effective in eco-friendly weeding. Flame thermal weeders, on the other hand, employ direct flames to sear and destroy weeds, providing a quick and efficient method of weed control. Both types cater to environmentally conscious farmers seeking alternatives to chemical herbicides for sustainable agriculture.</p></p>
