<p><p>The fire fighting foam market consists of three primary types: Protein Foam, Synthetic Foam, and Class A Foam. Protein Foam is derived from natural proteins, effective for hydrocarbon fires, providing excellent adhesion and film-forming abilities. Synthetic Foam, formulated from synthetic surfactants, offers improved performance for various fire types, with faster extinguishing capabilities. Class A Foam is specifically designed for fighting ordinary combustibles, enhancing water effectiveness and reducing surface tension for better penetration and rapid cooling. Each type serves distinct firefighting needs.</p></p>
