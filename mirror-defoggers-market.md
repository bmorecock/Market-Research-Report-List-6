<p><p>The Mirror Defoggers market can be categorized into two main types: Mesh Type and Vane Type. Mesh Type defoggers utilize a grid of fine wires embedded in the glass, providing uniform heat distribution to clear fog. In contrast, Vane Type defoggers use adjustable vanes to direct airflow over the mirror's surface, effectively preventing moisture buildup. Both types aim to enhance visibility and safety, appealing to consumers seeking solutions for foggy mirrors in various settings, particularly in vehicles and bathrooms.</p></p>
