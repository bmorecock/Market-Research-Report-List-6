<p><p>The CNG dispenser market is primarily divided into two types: Fast Fill and Time Fill. Fast Fill dispensers allow for rapid refueling of CNG vehicles, making them ideal for retail and public filling stations where quick service is essential. In contrast, Time Fill dispensers are designed for overnight or extended refueling, typically used at fleet facilities where vehicles can be filled during off-hours, ensuring a full tank by morning. Each type serves distinct operational needs within the CNG ecosystem.</p></p>
