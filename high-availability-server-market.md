<p><p>High Availability (HA) Server Market consists of five availability levels, each reflecting increasing redundancy and fault tolerance. </p><p>- **Level 1**: Single server; minimal redundancy.  </p><p>- **Level 2**: Redundant components; basic failover.  </p><p>- **Level 3**: Active-passive setups; limited downtime capabilities.  </p><p>- **Level 4**: Active-active configurations; robust failover mechanisms.  </p><p>- **Level 5**: Complete fault tolerance; real-time data replication and maintenance without downtime. </p><p>These levels cater to varying business continuity needs and operational criticality.</p></p>
