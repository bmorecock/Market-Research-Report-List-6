<p><p>The portable fire extinguisher market encompasses various types, including dry powder, foam, and carbon dioxide (CO2) extinguishers. Dry powder extinguishers are versatile, effective against flammable solid, liquid, and gas fires, making them suitable for diverse environments. Foam extinguishers are ideal for flammable liquid fires, forming a barrier that suppresses flames. CO2 extinguishers are effective for electrical and flammable liquid fires, displacing oxygen to extinguish flames without leaving residue. Each type serves specific fire scenarios, enhancing safety and response effectiveness.</p></p>
